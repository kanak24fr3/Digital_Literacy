Project Report: Task 3 - Digital Literacy & Google Workspace
For this task, I utilized Google Forms and Google Sheets to design and manage a "Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz." This platform was chosen for its seamless integration, allowing for real-time data collection and automated organization of responses. I built a comprehensive five-question assessment that incorporates diverse question types, including Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs) to test quick recognition of security protocols (like HTTPS and 2FA) and Short Answer questions to evaluate critical thinking regarding phishing red flags and digital footprints.
Practicing with these tools involved configuring validation rules for short answers and linking the form to a live Google Sheet to monitor results. Academically, mastering these platforms is highly beneficial; Google Forms simplifies the process of gathering primary data for research projects or surveys, while Google Sheets provides a robust environment for data analysis and visualization. These tools enhance my ability to collaborate on group assignments, manage large datasets efficiently, and present information in a structured, professional format. Ultimately, these skills are essential for maintaining academic integrity and staying organized in a digitally-driven learning environment.
